Animal Crossing: Gamecube Edition

There are many different ways to play Animal Crossing -- and there really is no right or wrong way. However, there are some basic objectives you can pursue in your daily life:

    Catch all fish and insects and donate them to the museum
    Find all fossils and complete the exhibit at the museum
    Find all paintings and complete the paintings exhibit at the museum
    Collect all of K. K. Slider's songs.
    Compete for the highest HRA rating for your house.
    Participate in each calendar event.
    Collect all hidden NES games -note that there are some games, like Legend of Zelda, that will only be available through special Nintendo events-
    Visit as many different villages as possible and trade items with friends.
    Live a harmonious life with the animals in your village and make sure that your village is an ideal place to live and move into.
